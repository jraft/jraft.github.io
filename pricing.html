Can IÂ trial Jraft before paying?
Sure! You can test out Jraft on our free plan with full access to our basic features. Your publication will have a 1 person user limit, a tryjraft.com domain and limited features, but you can purchase a plan to link a custom domain, more staff, collaborative editing, site comments and more.
How are 'active staff users' calculated?
Active staff users refers to the total number of team members invited to Jraft whom you collaborate with. For the purposes of billing, we only count users who are not suspended.
My publication relies on a lot of third party writer submissions, IÂ can't pay for a seat every time we publish one!
This isn't a problem, Jraft only counts active users as part of your pricing. If your publication is mostly driven by non full-time staff, simply suspend their accounts (their articles will stay up â€” they just can't login) and that will free up another seat.
How much traffic can the hosting handle?
Jraft hosting scales automatically to handle billions of concurrent visits. All site plans serve sites through our Amazon's Cloudfront CDN, loading sites in milliseconds.
Can I cancel my account plan at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your account at any time. If you do, you'll be downgraded to the free plan, where your publication will be delivered through a tryjraft.com domain and premium features like commenting will be disabled. You won't be locked out of any sites that have site plans set up, even if you're on the free plan.
Are Jraft publications SEO-friendly?
Yes! All content on Jraft publications is rendered on our servers and served through SSL, so your pages will be searchable and prioritized by Google. You can also edit meta and OpenGraph tags to optimise each page.

We offer fast email support to paid accounts and prioritized help for publisher accounts. Community support (forum.tryjraft.com) is available to free accounts.
